We Invested in Your Success

FRESHWORKS provide market leading IT support services, and have done
since 2010; it’s what we do and it’s what we do well. Through our network of
offices, we provide local IT support, Hosted Telephony Support and ERP
Solutions to over 900 SME clients, but it’s how we deliver it that is most
important.

We have the highest levels of technical expertise and accreditations to
resolve your issues, but we also have a genuine passion for understanding
your organisation, its goals and challenges. We don’t just fix your problems,
we find ways to improve your systems and positively impact your team and
business.

Whether you have no in-house IT expertise, or you are an IT Manager
looking for additional help, we will build a service that meets your specific
needs and priorities. We provide you with the advice and guidance that you
need to take advantage of ever evolving technology, and help you to plan
your IT Roadmap for the future.

                   
Our People

Our purpose is to provide the very best IT support service to your business and we know that
relies on our people. We really are a big family and we’re happy to admit to a healthy
obsession with selecting and keeping the right people because we know it makes the
difference. We have a number of team members who have moved on from Complete I.T. over
the years but quickly realised their mistake and come back.

So what should you expect from our people?

Our people will take an active interest in your business and its success, as though they were
an employee. They are all friendly, positive and proactive, finding ways to improve your use of
technology, and they always speak in plain English. All in all, not just the technical expert you
might have in mind.
Our Approach

You have a company to run. You know your business operations, objectives and what
you need to do to meet your goals. So why is IT absorbing more and more of your time and resources?

IT plays a crucial role in your day-to-day operations and you need a reliable, dependable
system, one that grows with you, adapts to your changing requirements, and delivers the
services and capabilities that you and your customers need, whenever and wherever you
need them.

We’ve been helping organisations across all market sectors manage and enhance their IT
infrastructures for over 25 years, taking away the pain of supporting IT systems, and freeing
up your team members to do their job. With a team of over 140 technical experts located in
our offices, we have years of experience with all technologies and many organisations.

But more importantly we listen, we understand your specific requirements, your culture, how
you want things to happen, and then we provide a service aligned to this. You’ll feel that we
are an extension of your organisation, a team that shares your challenges and cares about
fixing them. They are just some of the reasons clients choose us and, more importantly, stay
with us.
